Restaurants

Lindau

There are many excellent restaurants on Lindau Island. You can find a variety on Google Maps, but here are some of our favourites.

  • Hamma Bäckerei - A good local bakery with a variety of breads and pastries located on Maximilianstraße 11. View on map.
  • Restaurant Thung Thong - Great Thai food, offering lunch menus and delicious Pad Thai. View on map.
  • Pizzeria & Eiscafé Piccola - A really good quality pizza for little money. Take away and it at the lake side. View on map.
  • Restaurant Street Khazana - This one offers some Indian Street Food. Try Dosas, they are their speciality. View on map.
  • Restaurant Vina Haus - This one is our most favourite. They offer a variety of Vietnamese dishes, including Pho and Bun. They have really good offers for lunch during the week. View on map.
  • Restaurant Engel - Ideal for those wanting to experience regional cuisine, located on Schafgasse 4. View on map.
  • Restaurant zum Alten Rathaus - Another traditional restaurant located on Salzgasse 2. View on map.

Bregenz

If you find yourself in Bregenz, consider these highly recommended dining options:

  • Sakura Sushi Bar - One of the best sushi restaurants in the region. Try the "Dragon Roll." or "Rainbow Roll." View on map.
  • Goldener Hirschen - If you are looking for a traditional Austrian restaurant, this is the place to go. Käsespätzle is a must-try. View on map.
  • BAHI café & coworking - The best coffee in Bregenz. View on map.
  • Backery Mangold - It is a bakery, so don't expect a good coffee there. But they have some unique pastries. Try the Bienenstichcroissant. View on map.

Michelin Star Restaurants

If you are looking for a special dining experience or celebrating a special ocassion, there are several Michelin-starred restaurants in the region. Just look up directly on the Michelin Guide website.

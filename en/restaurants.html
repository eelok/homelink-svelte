Restaurants

Restaurants in Lindau

There are many good restaurants on Lindau Island.

  • Restaurant Thung Thong. In the neighboring building, there is a good Thai restaurant, Thung Thong, which offers many delicious Thai dishes.
  • Pizzeria IL Leone. The Pizzeria IL Leone on Vordere Metzgergasse 14 makes delicious pizzas.
  • Restaurant Street Khazana. We also recommend the Indian restaurant Street Khazana (Schmiedgasse 8).
  • Restaurant Vina Haus. The Vietnamese restaurant Vina Haus (Langenweg 24). Not on the island, but nearby.
  • Restaurant Engel. If you want to try regional cuisine, we recommend the Restaurant Engel (Schafgasse 4).
  • Restaurant zum Alten Rathaus. The Restaurant zum Alten Rathaus (Salzgasse 2) is also very good.

Restaurants in Bregenz

If you are in Bregenz, we recommend the following restaurants:

  • Sakura Sushi Bar. A restaurant with Japanese cuisine.
  • Goldener Hirschen. A traditional restaurant with Austrian cuisine.

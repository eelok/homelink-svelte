Restaurants

Lindau

There are many excellent restaurants on Lindau Island. You can find a variety on Google Maps, but here are some of our favourites.

  • Hamma Bäckerei - A local bakery for your morning coffee and pastry, located on Maximilianstraße 11. View on map.
  • Restaurant Thung Thong - A delightful Thai restaurant in the neighboring building offering a variety of delicious Thai dishes. View on map.
  • Pizzeria & Eiscafé Piccola - Known for crafting delicious pizzas, located on Vordere Metzgergasse 14. View on map.
  • Restaurant Street Khazana - A must-visit for lovers of Indian cuisine, located on Schmiedgasse 8. View on map.
  • Restaurant Vina Haus - A Vietnamese restaurant off the island at Langenweg 24, offering traditional flavors in a nearby setting. View on map.
  • Restaurant Engel - Ideal for those wanting to experience regional cuisine, located on Schafgasse 4. View on map.
  • Restaurant zum Alten Rathaus - Highly recommended for its high-quality offerings, situated on Salzgasse 2. View on map.

Bregenz

If you find yourself in Bregenz, consider these highly recommended dining options:

  • Sakura Sushi Bar - Offers an authentic Japanese dining experience. View on map.
  • Goldener Hirschen - A traditional restaurant renowned for its Austrian cuisine. View on map.
  • BAHI café & coworking - The best coffee in Bregenz. View on map.
  • Backery Mangold - Try the Bienenstichcroissant. View on map.

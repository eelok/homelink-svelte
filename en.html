Welcome to our apartment!

We are delighted that you have chosen our apartment for your stay in Lindau. We hope you will feel as comfortable here as we do and wish you a wonderful time at Lake Constance!

Keys

There are two keys: one for the front door and one for the apartment door. The key for the apartment door has a round end, while the key for the front door has a square end.

Apartment

Our apartment is located on the second floor on the right side. Please note that the building does not have an elevator, so there are a few stairs to climb.

The apartment has four rooms. The following are available to you:

  • Living room: with sofa, TV, desk, and floor lamp.
  • Dining room: with dining table, radio, chest of drawers, smart watch/speaker, and robotic vacuum.
  • Bedroom: with double bed and wardrobe. We will clear a section of the wardrobe for you. Clean bed linen and 2 sets of towels will be provided on the bed. Additional towels can be found in the chest of drawers in the dining room.
  • Kitchen: equipped with dishwasher, washer-dryer, and coffee machine. You are welcome to use all the ingredients for making muesli and everything in the fridge. Please do not wash knives, pots, and pans in the dishwasher. You will find detergent for the dishwasher and the washing machine in the storage cabinet in the hallway.
  • Balcony: with table and two chairs. You do not need to water the plants in winter.
  • Office: The office contains a desk, an office chair, and a floor lamp. The Wi-Fi printer, which you can use, is located in the hallway.
  • Bathroom and WC: You will find additional toilet paper in the bathroom cabinet.
  • Hallway: In the hallway, there is a wardrobe where you can hang your jackets and a shoe cabinet where you can place your shoes. In the wardrobe, you will also find 2 umbrellas that you are welcome to use. In addition, a vacuum cleaner and a mop are available to you. Please remove your shoes when entering the apartment.

Wi-Fi

The Wi-Fi network is named Martin. You will find the access data in the hallway on the telephone shelf.

Garbage and Recycling

In the kitchen, there are 3 garbage bins. There are two small bins under the sink for organic waste and residual waste. There are 2 bins for plastics and paper, which stand separately in the kitchen. You can throw the garbage bags into the large garbage can in the courtyard. There are bins for organic waste, residual waste, and paper. Unfortunately, there is no bin for plastics, but you can exceptionally throw plastic waste into the residual waste or leave it in the apartment.

In the courtyard next to the basement, the garbage bins are located in a locked room. You can open the room with the key from the front door.

Shopping & Eating

For shopping, we recommend Netto at Marktplatz 5 or Rewe at Zeppelinstraße 6. Both are open from 7 AM to 8 PM and are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

There are many good restaurants in the region.

Public Transport, Excursions, and Sightseeing

The Lindau-Insel train station is only a 5-minute walk away. From there, there are trains to Friedrichshafen, Munich, Austria, and other directions. Nearby is also the Stadttheater bus stop. At the harbor, there are boats to Switzerland, Austria, and scenic cruises, which unfortunately do not operate in winter.

We recommend a trip to Bregenz and from there by cable car to the Pfänder. From there, you have a great view of Lake Constance and the mountains.

Meersburg is also a beautiful city that you can reach by public transport or by car.

Lindau offers many sights and excursion destinations.

Wellness

The Lindau-Therme is a beautiful wellness center with a sauna and swimming pool.

Markets

There is a weekly market by the small lake that offers regional products.

Advice and Help

If you have any questions about the neighborhood, local conditions, or the house, please do not hesitate to ring at our neighbors Adelina and Sander. Their apartment is directly opposite ours. They are very friendly and knowledgeable about the area. They are happy to help with any concerns or uncertainties.

Health

On the island, there are many doctors and pharmacies. If needed, we can happily provide a recommendation.

We wish you an unforgettable stay in Lindau and are always available if you have any questions.

Garbage and Recycling

You'll find bins for organic waste, residual waste, plastics, and paper in the kitchen. Larger garbage bags can be disposed of in the courtyard. The garbage room next to the basement can be opened with your front door key.

Tap Water

Tap water in Germany, including Lindau, is safe to drink. In fact, Lindau's water comes directly from Lake Constance and undergoes careful treatment without chemicals. Thus you don't have to buy bottled water.
